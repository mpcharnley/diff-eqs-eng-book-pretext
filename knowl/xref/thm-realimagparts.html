Theorem 2.2.6.

Take the equation
\begin{equation*} ay'' + by' + cy = 0 . \end{equation*}
If the characteristic equation has the roots \(\alpha \pm i \beta\) (when \(b^2 - 4ac < 0\)), then the general solution is
\begin{equation*} y = C_1 e^{\alpha x} \cos (\beta x) + C_2 e^{\alpha x} \sin (\beta x) . \end{equation*}
